Elements Of AI

I completed the University of Helsinki’s Elements of AI and Building AI courses to ground myself in both the “what/why” and the “how” of artificial intelligence. In Elements of AI, I learned what AI is, explored basics like problem-solving, machine learning, and neural networks, and reflected on real-world uses and limitations of AI in society. Then, in Building AI, I went deeper into practical methods—covering approaches such as optimization, reasoning, and learning—so I could choose suitable techniques for simple tasks and outline my own AI idea, with light Python where helpful.
